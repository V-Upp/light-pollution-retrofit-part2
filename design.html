Design

CAD model & dimensions

A parametric 27" hood sized for standard cobra-head fixtures. The geometry below is a rough-complete first pass — dimensions and shielding angles are still being adjusted as we run wind, thermal, and photometric checks.

Drawing no. CHF-27-RH-001 Rev 1.0 Date July 2026 Formats STEP · STL · SVG · Python (CadQuery)
Reason for design: the visor and side wings block high-angle light without fully enclosing the fixture, so airflow and photocell function are preserved.
Still being investigated: whether the current visor extension (50 mm) blocks enough high-angle glare without cutting too much usable roadway light.
Reason for design: checking all four views together makes it easier to catch asymmetry or interference issues early, before cutting a dimensioned drawing.
Still being investigated: whether the top-view mounting slot spacing lines up with common tenon bracket hole patterns across manufacturers.

Notes

  • All dimensions in millimeters, with inches in parentheses.
  • Hood is symmetrical left to right.
  • Amber panel is removable for cleaning and maintenance.
  • Photocell on top of the fixture must remain unobstructed.
Mounting method

Clamp-on, no drilling

The hood attaches with a separate bracket assembly at the rear, sized to grip the fixture's existing mounting arm.

Rear clamp brackets

Two L-shaped stainless steel plates, each with a curved notch sized to grip a 1¼"–2" OD tenon arm.

Center safety bracket

One optional center bracket adds a secondary attachment point without requiring any drilling.

Ventilation slots

Four slot cutouts (25 × 75 mm, two per side) subtracted through the shell to reduce added wind load.

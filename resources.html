Terminology

Lighting terms used on this site

Full-cutoff

A fixture classification meaning no light is emitted above horizontal — none aimed at the sky.

BUG rating

Backlight, Uplight, Glare — an IES rating system describing how a fixture distributes light in different directions.

Light trespass

Light landing somewhere it isn't wanted or needed, such as a neighboring yard or window.

Sky glow

The brightening of the night sky caused by light scattering off particles and moisture in the atmosphere.

Color temperature (CCT)

A measure, in kelvin, of how warm or cool a light source appears — higher values mean bluer light.

EPA (effective projected area)

A structural engineering term for the effective flat surface area a shape presents to wind, used to estimate wind loading.
